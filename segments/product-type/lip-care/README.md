# Lip Care Products

Products specifically formulated for lip care and protection.

## Overview

Lip care products address the unique needs of lip skin, which is more delicate and prone to dryness than other facial areas. These products provide:

- **Hydration**: Moisture and prevention of dryness
- **Protection**: Shielding from environmental factors (sun, wind, cold)
- **Treatment**: Healing for chapped or damaged lips
- **Enhancement**: Color and aesthetic appeal (in some formulations)

## Product Categories

### Lip Balms
Directory: `lip-balms/`

Essential lip care products that provide moisture, protection, and comfort to the delicate lip skin. Includes classic lip balms (stick, pot, tube formats), medicated lip balms, SPF lip balms, tinted lip balms, natural/organic lip balms, flavored lip balms, overnight lip masks, and luxury lip balms.

### Lip Treatments
Directory: `lip-treatments/`

Intensive and specialized lip care products beyond basic moisturization, targeting specific concerns. Includes lip plumpers, overnight lip masks, lip scrubs, anti-aging lip treatments, lip serums, lip oils, brightening treatments, healing treatments, lip primers, lip patches, and color-correcting treatments.

## Market Relevance

Lip care represents a specialized niche in the skincare market, with products ranging from basic protection to premium treatment formulations. The segment appeals to consumers seeking both functional protection and cosmetic enhancement, with high repurchase frequency across universal demographics.
