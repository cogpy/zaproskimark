# Body Care Products

Products formulated for body skincare, providing hydration and specialized care for different body areas.

## Product Categories

### Body Lotion
Directory: `body-lotion/`

Hydrating products formulated for body skin, providing moisture and nourishment to larger skin areas. Includes daily body lotions, rich body creams, body butters, lightweight body gels, firming body lotions, and specialized treatment lotions for various skin concerns.

### Foot and Hand Cream
Directory: `foot-hand-cream/`

Specialized moisturizers for hands and feet, addressing the unique needs of these areas which are often exposed to environmental stressors and frequent washing. Includes daily hand creams, intensive hand creams, hand balms, working hands creams, anti-aging hand creams, daily foot creams, intensive foot creams, cracked heel treatments, cooling foot gels, anti-fungal foot creams, and cuticle care products.

### Other Body Care Products
Directory: `other-body-care/`

Additional body treatments including body scrubs and exfoliators (sugar, salt, coffee, body polishes), body oils (dry oils, rich nourishing oils, massage oils, multi-purpose oils), body butters, firming and toning products, specialty area treatments (bust, arm, thigh creams), body masks, body peels, after-sun care, self-tanning products, body serums, in-shower products, deodorants, and body powders.

## Market Relevance

Body care products cater to consumers seeking comprehensive skincare solutions beyond facial care, addressing the need for whole-body skin health and appearance.
