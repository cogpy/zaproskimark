# Cleansers

Products designed to remove dirt, oil, makeup, and impurities from facial skin while maintaining the skin's natural protective barrier.

## Product Categories

### Foam Cleansers
Lightweight, airy formulations that create a rich lather when mixed with water. Ideal for normal to oily skin types.

### Gel Cleansers
Clear or translucent formulations with a smooth, gel-like consistency. Excellent for oily and combination skin types.

### Cream Cleansers
Rich, creamy formulations that provide gentle cleansing while maintaining moisture. Best suited for dry and sensitive skin.

### Micellar Water
No-rinse cleansing water containing micelles that attract and remove dirt, oil, and makeup. Suitable for all skin types.

### Oil Cleansers
Oil-based formulations that effectively remove makeup and sunscreen while nourishing the skin. Particularly effective for dry skin and as a first step in double cleansing.

### Cleansing Balms
Solid, balm-like formulations that emulsify upon contact with water. Excellent for removing heavy makeup and sunscreen.

### Exfoliating Cleansers
Cleansers formulated with physical or chemical exfoliants to remove dead skin cells while cleansing. Used 2-3 times per week.

## Key Ingredients

### pH-Balanced Formulations
Cleansers formulated to match the skin's natural pH (4.5-5.5) to maintain the protective acid mantle.

### Surfactants
Cleansing agents that help remove dirt and oil. Professional formulations use gentle surfactants to minimize irritation.

### Moisturizing Agents
Ingredients like glycerin, hyaluronic acid, and ceramides that help maintain skin hydration during cleansing.

### Active Ingredients
- **Salicylic Acid**: For acne-prone skin
- **Glycolic Acid**: For gentle exfoliation
- **Niacinamide**: For brightening and pore refinement
- **Vitamin C**: For antioxidant protection

## Usage Guidelines

### Daily Cleansing Routine
- **Morning**: Single cleanse to remove overnight oil production
- **Evening**: Double cleanse to remove makeup, sunscreen, and daily impurities

### Proper Technique
1. Wet face with lukewarm water
2. Apply cleanser to fingertips or cleansing device
3. Gently massage in circular motions for 30-60 seconds
4. Rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water
5. Pat dry with a clean towel
