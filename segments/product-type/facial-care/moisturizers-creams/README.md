# Moisturizers and Creams

Hydrating products designed to maintain skin moisture, strengthen the skin barrier, and protect against environmental stressors.

## Product Categories

### Day Moisturizers
Lightweight formulations designed for daytime use, often with SPF protection and antioxidants.

#### Key Features
- Light to medium texture
- Fast absorption
- Often contain SPF
- Protection against environmental stressors
- Suitable for makeup application

### Night Creams
Rich, intensive formulations designed to nourish and repair skin during sleep.

#### Key Features
- Heavier, more emollient texture
- Time-release active ingredients
- Focus on repair and regeneration
- No SPF required
- Higher concentration of active ingredients

### Lightweight Lotions
Fluid, easily absorbed formulations suitable for normal to oily skin types.

#### Key Features
- Watery to gel-cream texture
- Quick absorption
- Non-greasy finish
- Ideal for humid climates
- Suitable for layering

### Rich Creams
Dense, nourishing formulations for dry and mature skin types.

#### Key Features
- Thick, luxurious texture
- Long-lasting hydration
- Barrier repair properties
- Ideal for cold, dry climates
- Anti-aging benefits

### Gel Moisturizers
Water-based, oil-free formulations perfect for oily and acne-prone skin.

#### Key Features
- Lightweight, cooling texture
- Non-comedogenic
- Oil-free formulation
- Mattifying effect
- Suitable for hot, humid weather

### Whipped Creams
Airy, mousse-like formulations that provide hydration without heaviness.

#### Key Features
- Fluffy, cloud-like texture
- Medium hydration level
- Pleasant sensory experience
- Suitable for combination skin
- Easy to apply and spread

## Key Ingredients

### Humectants
Draw moisture into the skin:
- **Hyaluronic Acid**: Holds up to 1000x its weight in water
- **Glycerin**: Attracts and retains moisture
- **Panthenol (Vitamin B5)**: Hydrates and soothes
- **Urea**: Moisturizes and gently exfoliates

### Emollients
Soften and smooth the skin:
- **Squalane**: Lightweight, non-greasy moisture
- **Jojoba Oil**: Mimics skin's natural sebum
- **Shea Butter**: Rich, nourishing moisture
- **Ceramides**: Strengthen skin barrier

### Occlusives
Seal in moisture:
- **Petrolatum**: Highly effective barrier
- **Dimethicone**: Silky, protective layer
- **Beeswax**: Natural sealing agent
- **Lanolin**: Intensive moisture retention

### Active Ingredients
- **Niacinamide**: Reduces inflammation, brightens
- **Retinol**: Anti-aging, cell turnover
- **Vitamin C**: Antioxidant, brightening
- **Peptides**: Collagen production, firming
- **Alpha Hydroxy Acids (AHAs)**: Gentle exfoliation
