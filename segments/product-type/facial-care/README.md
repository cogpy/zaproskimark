# Facial Care Products

Products designed to remove dirt, oil, and impurities from facial skin, maintain skin moisture, and provide targeted treatments.

## Product Categories

### Cleansers
Directory: `cleansers/`

Products designed to remove dirt, oil, and impurities from facial skin while maintaining the skin's natural protective barrier. Includes foam cleansers, gel cleansers, cream cleansers, micellar water, oil cleansers, cleansing balms, and exfoliating cleansers.

### Moisturizers and Creams
Directory: `moisturizers-creams/`

Hydrating products designed to maintain skin moisture and barrier function. Includes day moisturizers, night creams, lightweight lotions, rich creams, gel moisturizers, and whipped creams formulated with humectants, emollients, and occlusives.

### Serums and Essence
Directory: `serums-essence/`

Concentrated treatments formulated with active ingredients for targeted skin concerns. Includes hydrating serums, anti-aging serums, brightening serums, antioxidant serums, treatment serums, and essences with high concentrations of active ingredients.

### Toners
Directory: `toners/`

Products that help balance skin pH and prepare skin for subsequent treatments. Includes hydrating toners, exfoliating toners, pH-balancing toners, treatment toners, essence toners, and astringent toners.

### Face Masks
Directory: `face-masks/`

Treatment products applied to the face for specific skincare benefits. Includes sheet masks, clay masks, cream masks, gel masks, peel-off masks, sleeping masks, exfoliating masks, and bubble/foam masks.

### Other Facial Care Products
Directory: `other-facial-care/`

Additional facial treatments and specialized care products not covered in the main categories. Includes eye creams and treatments, facial oils, face mists and sprays, facial peels, facial sunscreens, spot treatments, facial essences, ampoules, and various specialized treatments.

## Market Relevance

Facial care products represent a significant portion of the skincare market, addressing various skin concerns from basic cleansing to advanced anti-aging treatments.
